Quick Tools is a completely offline application.
                    
                    Data Storage:
                    - All data (notes, preferences) are stored locally on your device only
                    - No data is sent to any servers
                    - No data is shared with third parties
                    
                    Permissions:
                    - This app does not require any special permissions
                    - No access to photos, contacts, location, etc.
                    
                    Data Deletion:
                    - All data can be removed by deleting the app
                    - Notes are stored only on your current device
